Training the Next Generation

Education

From undergraduate minors to intensive bootcamps, QuAIL offers pathways for every stage of learning.

01

Minor of Undergraduate Degrees

An interdisciplinary minor in quantum computing and AI available to undergraduate students across engineering, science, mathematics, and computer science programs. Designed to give the next generation of scientists and engineers foundational literacy in quantum technologies.

Undergraduate
02

Master Degree

A graduate-level master's program focused on quantum computing, quantum sensing, and AI integration. Prepares students for careers in academia, national laboratories, and industry through rigorous coursework, research projects, and collaboration with QuAIL network partners.

Graduate
03

Graduate Certificate

A flexible certificate program for professionals and graduate students seeking focused training in quantum computing and AI without committing to a full degree. Ideal for those looking to upskill or pivot into quantum-adjacent fields.

Graduate · Professional
04

Summer School

An intensive summer program bringing together students and early-career researchers for hands-on training in quantum computing, sensing, and AI methodologies. Features lectures from leading experts, lab visits, and collaborative projects.

Summer · All Levels
05

Bootcamp

Short-form, immersive bootcamp experiences designed to rapidly upskill scientists and engineers in practical quantum computing and AI tools and techniques. Accessible to participants from any technical background, with no prior quantum experience required.

Short-Term · Professional
