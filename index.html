About National QuAIL

National Quantum AI Laboratory (National QuAIL) is a national network dedicated to identifying near-term and long-term applications of quantum computing and quantum sensing.

It brings together experts in quantum mechanics, quantum computing, quantum sensing, artificial intelligence, computer science, mathematics, science, and engineering from academia, national labs, industry, and NGOs.

By breaking traditional disciplinary silos, QuAIL aims to address intractable scientific and engineering challenges through the orchestration of CPU-, GPU-, and QPU-based computing.

To democratize access to quantum computing, QuAIL will leverage AI to enable scientists and engineers without deep quantum expertise to discover and utilize quantum algorithms, optimize workflows, and advance quantum error correction and hybrid computing frameworks.

Vision

Empower quantum computing using AI.

Mission

  • Build a national network dedicated to identifying near-term and long-term applications of quantum computing and quantum sensing across academia, national laboratories, industry, and NGOs.
  • Break traditional disciplinary silos to address intractable scientific and engineering challenges through the orchestration of CPU-, GPU-, and QPU-based computing.
  • Democratize access to quantum computing by leveraging AI to enable scientists and engineers to discover and utilize quantum algorithms and optimize workflows.
